The three portions that are making Tricell up are the :

  1. Pharmauceutical,
  2. Natural Resources and

 1. Pharmaceutial Research
The pharmaceutical cell was originally founded after the medicinal herbs were collected from Africa by Henry Travis in the late 1900’s.
 

 
 2. Natural Resources
The natural resources cell is known for it's extraction of fossil fuels, operating an extensive oil facility in West Africa.
 

 
 3. Shippping
The shipping cell has foundations in the Travis family's business trade with Asia, founded by Henry’s father Thomas Travis in 1935.
