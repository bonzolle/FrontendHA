  Background
Groot: The Sentient Tree: Groot is a Flora Colossus, a massive, sentient, tree-like being whose exterior is composed of durable, regenerative wood. He is defined by his unwavering loyalty and gentle spirit, often serving as the moral heart of the unpredictable Guardians of the Galaxy. Despite his immense strength and ability to rapidly grow and manipulate his branches for attack or defense, he is only capable of speaking the phrase: "I am Groot." Though his vocabulary is limited, his best friend, the cynical Rocket Raccoon, can perfectly understand every complex emotion, plan, or simple thought conveyed through the unique tone and inflection of his three words. Groot is the ultimate symbol of self-sacrifice, having proven time and again that he will give everything to protect his chosen family.
  Strengths
Superhuman Strength and Durability: Groot is immensely strong, capable of lifting heavy objects and overpowering powerful enemies. His body, composed of dense wood, also grants him incredible resistance to damage.

Regeneration/Regrowth: This is his signature ability. Groot can heal almost instantly from severe injuries and can completely regrow from a single twig or sapling if his body is destroyed. This makes him virtually immortal.
  Weaknesses
Limited Communication Ability: His most obvious weakness is his inability to speak more than "I am Groot." This makes it nearly impossible for new allies (and the audience) to understand his thoughts, plans, or emotions without Rocket as a translator.

Vulnerability to Ranged/Aerial Attacks: Because he is so large and lacks quick movement, enemies who can attack from long range or the air can easily land damage on him.
