  Background
Oliver was the son of the late Robert Queen and Moira Queen, the older paternal half-brother of Emiko Adachi, the older maternal half-brother of Thea Queen, the husband of Felicity Smoak, and the father of William Clayton and Mia Queen. He was also the ex-boyfriend/good friend of the late Laurel Lance, Sara Lance, and Shado. He was also the ex-lover of Samantha Clayton. He was also the ex-boyfriend turned enemy of Helena Bertinelli. He was also the best friend of Tommy Merlyn, John Diggle and Barry Allen, and a good friend of Quentin Lance, Roy Harper, Lyla Michaels, Anatoly Knyazev, Ray Palmer, Kara Danvers, Rene Ramirez, Dinah Drake, and Curtis Holt.

Oliver was lost from society after his family's yacht sank and made it to the island of Lian Yu, where he went on a mission of survival and self-discovery, learning skills that include, and aren't limited to, archery, swordsmanship, hand-hand combat, etc. He traveled to China and Russia on missions as an agent of A.R.G.U.S. for some time, became a member of the Solntsevskaya Bratva known as Kapot (Russian for Car Hood), and the murderous vigilante Luchnik/Kapiushon (Russian for Archer/Hood). After being presumed dead and lost at sea for five years, Oliver returned home with a mission to save his city from crime and corruption. He began his crusade as an archer who would eventually become known as the Hood, Starling City Vigilante[4][5][6], or simply the Vigilante. As the Hood, Oliver was willing to use lethal force by targeting members on his father's list.

  Weaknesses
Physical Vulnerability:

As a normal human, Oliver is susceptible to physical harm from stronger opponents. While he is a skilled archer and martial artist, he often relies on ranged attacks to compensate for his physical limitations.

Emotional and Psychological Weaknesses: Trauma and Mental Health:

Oliver's experiences on Lian Yu, particularly the loss of loved ones, have left him with emotional scars and a tendency towards depression and self-destructive behavior.

Difficulty Forming Trusting Relationships:

Oliver's past betrayals and experiences with loss make it challenging for him to fully trust others, even those closest to him.

Recklessness and Impulsivity:

Oliver's past actions and choices, particularly when dealing with threats, often showcase a degree of recklessness and a willingness to take extreme measures.

Moral Ambiguity:

While he strives to be a hero, Oliver's past actions, including brutal methods and torture, demonstrate a darker side and a tendency towards moral ambiguity.

Alcoholism:

Oliver struggled with alcoholism as a coping mechanism for dealing with trauma and grief.

Paranoia:

Oliver's past experiences have also led to paranoia and a tendency to isolate himself when facing perceived threats.
